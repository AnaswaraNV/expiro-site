Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-17

Expiro keeps your product-tracking data on your device. We do not require an account. If crash reporting is enabled, limited technical diagnostic data may be sent to Sentry to help fix bugs.

What Expiro stores

Expiro stores your product details, reminder settings, and app preferences on your device so the app can work.

What Expiro collects

Expiro does not require an account.

Expiro does not upload your product shelf, notes, or reminder list to Expiro servers as part of normal app use.

If crash diagnostics are enabled for a build, technical error information may be sent to Sentry, our crash reporting provider, to help us find and fix app stability problems.

Permissions

Expiro may ask for notification permission so it can deliver local reminder alerts before a product expires.

If you do not allow notifications, the app still works, but reminder alerts will stay off.

How we use information

We use locally stored data to show your shelf, calculate expiry timing, and manage reminders on your device.

If crash diagnostics are enabled, we use diagnostic information only to monitor stability and improve reliability.

Sharing

Expiro does not sell your personal data.

If crash diagnostics are enabled, technical diagnostic data may be processed by Sentry on our behalf.

Retention

Your Expiro product data stays on your device until you delete it or remove the app.

Your choices

You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings.

You can remove Expiro from your device to delete the app data stored locally.
