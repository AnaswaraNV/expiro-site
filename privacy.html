# Expiro Privacy Policy Effective date: 2026-03-17 Expiro is a local-first app for tracking product expiry dates. ## What Expiro stores Expiro stores your product details, reminder settings, and app preferences on your device so the app can work. ## What Expiro collects Expiro does not require an account. Expiro does not upload your product shelf, notes, or reminder list to Expiro servers as part of normal app use. If crash diagnostics are enabled for a build, technical error information may be sent to Sentry, our crash reporting provider, to help us find and fix app stability problems. This diagnostic information may include items such as app version, device type, operating system version, crash logs, and error context. ## Permissions Expiro may ask for notification permission so it can deliver local reminder alerts before a product expires. If you do not allow notifications, the app still works, but reminder alerts will stay off. ## How we use information We use locally stored data to show your shelf, calculate expiry timing, and manage reminders on your device. If crash diagnostics are enabled, we use diagnostic information only to monitor stability, investigate bugs, and improve reliability. ## Sharing Expiro does not sell your personal data. If crash diagnostics are enabled, technical diagnostic data may be processed by Sentry on our behalf for error monitoring and debugging. ## Retention Your Expiro product data stays on your device until you change it, delete it, or remove the app. Diagnostic events sent to Sentry, when enabled, are retained under the retention settings configured for the Expiro Sentry project. ## Your choices You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings. You can remove Expiro from your device to delete the app data stored locally on that device. ## Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@expiro.app --- ## Short Web Intro Blurb Use this if your website needs a tiny summary above the full policy: Expiro keeps your product-tracking data on your device.
